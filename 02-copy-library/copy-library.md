### Standalone Reiki Session
**Short version:**
Pure energy work. No massage. A session for the energetic body — clearing, recalibrating, restoring what has been scattered or depleted. Offered lying down, fully clothed.

**Full description:**
Some people come specifically for Reiki — not because they do not want bodywork, but because what they are carrying lives in a layer that hands alone cannot reach.

A standalone Reiki session works entirely with the energetic body. You are fully clothed, lying down, held in a field of intentional presence. What gets moved in a Reiki session often cannot be explained in physical terms — and does not need to be. The body knows what happened, even when the mind has not caught up yet.

This session is for people in the middle of something hard. People in transition — grief, diagnosis, burnout, the strange disorientation of major change. People who already know what Reiki is and what it does for them. And sometimes, people who have never experienced it but feel drawn to it anyway. That pull is usually worth following.

**Never use:**
journey · healing journey · transformative · transformation · holistic wellness · self-care · competitors · fix / fixed / fixing · business problem · visibility · solution · invest in yourself · game-changer · life-changing · empower / empowering · align / alignment (in a spiritual context) · manifest / manifestation · energy healing (use "energy work") · mind-body-spirit (triptych cliché) · practitioner (use "Anissa" or "she") · client (use "person" or "people" in prose where natural)

**Watch for drift in:**
- Second-person sections drifting into third-person
- Copy that tells the reader what they will feel instead of what they will receive
- Overlong sentences that bury the point (Anissa's voice is compressed, not expansive)
